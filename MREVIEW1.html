Movie Review: Tumbbad

Tumbbad (2018) is a groundbreaking masterpiece of Indian cinstronga that seamlessly blends folk horror, dark fantasy, and socio-political commentary. Directed by Rahi Anil Barve and Anand Gandhi, the film uses a fictionalized mythological framework to deliver a scathing critique of human greed across three distinct generations.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆(9.5/10)
