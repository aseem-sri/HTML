Creamy Garlic Pasta

This 10-Minute Creamy Garlic Pasta is the ultimate "emergency" meal for when you're hungry but short on time. It features tender noodles swirled in a silky, buttery sauce infused with the punchy aroma of fresh sautéed garlic. The base is a rich blend of heavy cream and melted Parmesan, creating a velvety coating that clings to every strand. Finished with a crack of black pepper and a hint of Italian herbs, it’s a restaurant-quality comfort dish that goes from pantry to plate in the time it takes to boil water. It’s simple, decadent, and incredibly satisfying.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Pasta: 250g (8 oz) spaghetti, penne, or angel hair.
  • Garlic: 3–4 cloves, finely minced (use more if you're a garlic lover!).
  • Dairy: 1 cup heavy cream (or 1/2 cup cream + 1/2 cup milk) and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fats: 2 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp olive oil.
  • Liquids: 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth (optional, for extra depth).
  • Seasoning: Salt, black pepper, and 1 tsp Italian herbs or fresh parsley.

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Cook Pasta:Boil pasta until al dente, reserving some starchy water before draining.

  3. Build the sauce:Stir in flour, then slowly whisk in cream and/or broth until smooth.
  4. Melt cheese:Add Parmesan and stir until melted and the sauce is creamy.
  5. Combine:Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce, adding reserved pasta water to reach desired consistency.
  6. Garnish:Finish with fresh parsley and extra cheese before serving

Want to level it up? You can easily add grilled chicken, sautéed mushrooms, or a handful of baby spinach at the very end for a fuller meal.

Grab a fork, dive in, and enjoy every creamy, garlicky bite!
